Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 5, 2023

Welcome to Fruit Adventure Club. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you use our mobile application and tell you about your privacy rights.

1. Information We Collect

Fruit Adventure Club is designed to respect your privacy. We collect minimal information necessary to provide you with a great gaming experience:

  • Game progress and preferences
  • Device information for app functionality and optimization
  • Anonymous usage statistics to improve gameplay

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Save your game progress
  • Improve the app's performance and features
  • Analyze gameplay patterns to enhance user experience
  • Provide technical support if needed

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. We may share anonymous, aggregated data for analytics purposes. We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights or the rights of others.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Choices

You can reset game data through your device settings. Please note this will erase your game progress. You may also disable certain device permissions through your device settings, though this may affect some app functionality.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
